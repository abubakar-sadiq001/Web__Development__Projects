Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection. optionally filled with your cheese, vegetables, or meals

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparations: minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, decided vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. Tou can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melting and bubbling, pour in the eggs.Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly running in the middlle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omolette.
  5. Fold and serve: As the omolette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and over the fillings. Left it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
